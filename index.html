About

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Hanyang University and will receive my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the same institution in February 2026. Since July 2018, I have been a member of the Cryptology & Algorithm Lab, advised by Professor Jae Hong Seo.

From January 2023 to January 2024 I was an ARAP scholar at the A*STAR Institute for Infocomm Research (Singapore), working with Dr. Khin Mi Mi Aung and Dr. Tan Yong Kiam.

My research sits at the intersection of applied mathematics, biometric security, and computer vision. I work on adversarial attacks against face recognition, biometric template protection, and certified robustness — bridging mathematical rigor with the practical security of deployed AI systems. See the research page for an overview of the threads, or the publications page for the full list of papers.

I have served as a reviewer for CVPR 2024–26, and have been recognized as a 1st Graduate Presidential Science Scholarship recipient (the only finalist in the mathematics field), with a Hanyang Outstanding Ph.D. Dissertation Award (2026) and several national cryptographic technology contest awards. I have led a National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF) project as Principal Investigator on secure biometric authentication.
