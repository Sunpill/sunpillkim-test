About

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Hanyang University and will receive my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the same institution in February 2026. Since July 2018, I have been a member of the Cryptology & Algorithm Lab, advised by Professor Jae Hong Seo.

From January 2023 to January 2024 I was an ARAP scholar at the A*STAR Institute for Infocomm Research (Singapore), working with Dr. Khin Mi Mi Aung and Dr. Tan Yong Kiam.

My work spans adversarial attacks on face recognition systems, biometric template protection, and certified robustness—bridging mathematical rigor with the practical security of deployed AI systems.
